Who Am I?

Hey! I'm Mudit Gupta. I was born in Georgia on March 17, 2001, and I've been living in the suburbs my whole life. I grew up with the gift of my parents having hundreds of friends. All the family around me molded me into the fun-loving person I am today. I bring my goofy (slightly immature) antics into every room I enter, and as I got older, I realized that the smiles I could put on other people's faces put a smile twice as big on mine. My passion for making others happy has driven me throughout my life. When I'm not working on something of my own, I often find myself helping someone else work on something of their own.

In highschool, my enthusiasm for robotics became my personality. I spent all four years invested in my robotics team, and we reached the international competition twice! After graduating from GSMST, I became a student at Georgia Tech. I'm working towards a Computer Science degree, focusing on artificial intelligence and modeling/simulation.
